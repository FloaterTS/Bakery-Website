About

Not so long ago, Flappy Pancake Bakery opened its first kiosk location in Towson, Maryland. From its inception, Flappy Pancake Bakery is known as bakery unique for our square pancakes and cakes. We take pride in using natural ingredients in our pancakes, cakes and desserts. Our treats are as deliciously wholesome as they are beautifully decorated. Choose from our signature pancakes, weekly and holiday specials or custom created desserts.

The Founder? Papa Sweet himself!

We are proud of our principles. Papa Sweet made sure that our bakery
will be unlike any other. It is his merit for our welcomed uniqueness.
